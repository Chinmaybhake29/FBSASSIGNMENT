How Make Tea

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Milk
  • Tea Leaves
  • Sugar
  • Tea Masala

Steps to Prepare

  1. Boil water in a pan.
  2. Add tea leaves and masala.
  3. Let it boil for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add milk and sugar.
  5. Boil again and strain into a cup.

Optional Toppings

  1. Mint Leaves
  2. Crushed Saffron
